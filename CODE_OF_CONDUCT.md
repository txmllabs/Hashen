# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (short)

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the Hashen community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, or other characteristics.

## Our standards

- **Positive**: Be respectful, use welcoming language, accept constructive criticism.
- **Unacceptable**: Harassment, trolling, insulting comments, public or private abuse, publishing others’ private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Community maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this code of conduct. Violations may be reported to **developer@txmllabs.com**. We will respond and take action we deem appropriate.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces (repos, issues, PRs) and when representing the project in public.

For the full Contributor Covenant, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
